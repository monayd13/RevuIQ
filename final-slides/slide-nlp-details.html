NLP Techniques Deep Dive

Core NLP Methods

1. Tokenization
Breaking text into individual words and sentences for analysis
Example: "Great food!" → ["Great", "food", "!"]
2. POS Tagging
Identifying parts of speech (nouns, verbs, adjectives)
Example: "food" → Noun, "great" → Adjective
3. Noun Phrase Extraction
Finding key topics and aspects mentioned in reviews
Example: "the food", "excellent service"
4. Polarity Scoring
Measuring sentiment on a scale from -1 (negative) to +1 (positive)
Example: "Amazing!" → +0.9, "Terrible" → -0.8
