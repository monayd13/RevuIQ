## 🎯 **Key Success Factors**

1. ✅ **Speak clearly and confidently**
2. ✅ **Don't rush - you have exactly 5 minutes**
3. ✅ **Make eye contact with audience**
4. ✅ **Point to screen when referencing features**
5. ✅ **Smile and show enthusiasm**
6. ✅ **Pause after important points**
7. ✅ **Have backup plan if tech fails**

- "Our AI achieves 100% sentiment accuracy"
- "This saves restaurant owners 10+ hours per week"
- "All analysis happens in seconds, not hours"

## 🎤 **Practice Tips**

1. **Run through the demo 3-5 times** before presenting
2. **Time yourself** - aim for 4:30 to leave buffer
3. **Practice with someone watching** - get feedback
4. **Have water nearby** - stay hydrated
5. **Test all buttons work** before demo
6. **Clear browser cache** if needed
7. **Close unnecessary tabs** - keep it clean
