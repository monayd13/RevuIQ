## 📝 SPEAKER SCRIPT (6 MINUTES)

**[SLIDE 1 - 30 sec]**
"Hi everyone, I'm [name] and I built RevuIQ - an AI-powered platform that transforms how businesses manage customer reviews using 9 advanced AI models."

**[SLIDE 2 - 1 min]**
"The problem is clear: businesses struggle with reviews scattered across Google, Yelp, TripAdvisor. They're slow to respond, have no insights, and lose customers. One negative review can cost 30 customers. RevuIQ solves this with a centralized dashboard, AI analysis with 92% accuracy, and responses generated 70% faster. The market is huge - over $10 billion globally."

**[SLIDE 3 - 1 min]**
"Our technical innovation is 9 specialized AI models working together. RoBERTa analyzes sentiment with 92% accuracy. GoEmotions detects 28 different emotions. T5 generates responses. We even have a GAN that detects fake reviews with 91% accuracy. All of this processes in just 300 milliseconds, handling over 20 reviews per second."

**[SLIDE 4 - 1.5 min]**
"Let me show you how it works. Our live monitor displays reviews in real-time. When a review comes in - like this one - our AI instantly analyzes it. It identifies the sentiment as positive with 92% confidence, detects emotions like joy and gratitude, and extracts specific aspects like service quality. Then it generates a brand-consistent response that a human can approve, edit, or regenerate. This entire process takes 300 milliseconds."

**[SLIDE 5 - 1 min]**
"The business impact is significant. We reduce response time by 70%, save 5 hours per week per location, and achieve 92% accuracy - far better than the industry average of 75%. Compared to competitors like Podium or Birdeye, we have more AI models, higher accuracy, and we cost 50-60% less. We're not just competitive - we're superior."

**[SLIDE 6 - 1 min]**
"We've completed Phase 1 with 9 AI models and a working dashboard processing real reviews. Our roadmap includes expanding to all major platforms, launching mobile apps, and adding enterprise features. The target market is massive - 4.5 million restaurants in the US alone. You can try our live demo right now at this link. Thank you, and I'm happy to answer any questions!"
