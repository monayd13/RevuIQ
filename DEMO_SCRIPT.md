# RevuIQ Demo Script (5 Minutes)

## 🎯 **Opening (30 seconds)**

"Hello everyone! Today I'm excited to show you **RevuIQ** - an AI-powered review management system that helps restaurants understand customer feedback and respond professionally using natural language processing."

---

## 📱 **1. Landing Page (30 seconds)**

*[Show homepage]*

"This is our landing page. RevuIQ solves a major problem for restaurants - managing reviews from multiple platforms is time-consuming and overwhelming. Our platform uses AI to:
- Analyze sentiment automatically
- Detect emotions in reviews
- Generate personalized responses
- Provide actionable insights"

*[Click "Get Started"]*

---

## 🔐 **2. Sign Up (20 seconds)**

*[Show signup page]*

"The signup process is simple and quick. Restaurant owners can create an account in seconds."

*[Fill in details and sign up]*

"Once signed up, you're taken directly to the restaurant management dashboard."

---

## 🏪 **3. Restaurant Management (30 seconds)**

*[Show restaurants page]*

"Here's our restaurant management dashboard. Let me show you with a real example - **Starbucks Santa Clara**."

*[Click "Add Restaurant" if needed, or show existing Starbucks]*

"We can manage multiple restaurant locations from one place. Each restaurant has its own analytics and review management."

---

## 📥 **4. Fetching Reviews (45 seconds)**

*[Click "Fetch Google Reviews" button]*

"Now, the magic happens. With one click, we fetch real reviews from Google Places API."

*[Wait for reviews to load]*

"Our AI immediately analyzes each review using advanced NLP:
- **Sentiment Analysis** - Is it positive, negative, or neutral?
- **Emotion Detection** - What emotions are customers feeling? Joy, anger, disappointment?
- **Aspect Extraction** - What are they talking about? Food quality, service, ambiance?"

"All of this happens automatically in seconds. No manual work required."

---

## 🤖 **5. AI-Generated Responses (1 minute)**

*[Navigate to "Approve Responses" tab]*

"Here's where it gets really powerful. For each review, our AI has already generated a personalized response."

*[Show a negative review and its AI response]*

"Look at this negative review. Our AI:
- Detected the negative sentiment
- Identified the specific issues mentioned
- Generated an empathetic, professional apology
- Offered to make things right"

*[Show a positive review and its AI response]*

"And for positive reviews, the AI creates grateful, warm responses that encourage customers to return."

"The restaurant owner can review these responses and approve them with one click. This saves hours of time every week."

*[Click approve on a response]*

---

## 📊 **6. View Analytics (1.5 minutes)**

*[Click "View Analytics" button]*

"Now let's see the real power - actionable insights."

*[Show analytics dashboard]*

### **Sentiment Distribution**
"At a glance, we can see:
- **80% of reviews are negative** - This is a red flag that needs immediate attention
- Only 20% positive
- The average rating is 1.8 stars"

### **Emotion Analysis**
"The emotion breakdown shows customers are feeling:
- **Sadness** and **Disappointment** are the top emotions
- This tells us customers had high expectations that weren't met"

### **Top Aspects**
"Our AI identified what customers are talking about:
- **Service issues** - Staff behavior problems
- **Food quality** - Complaints about freshness
- **Wait times** - Long delays"

### **Recent Reviews**
*[Scroll to show individual reviews with sentiment badges]*

"Each review is color-coded:
- 🔴 **Red** = Negative (immediate action needed)
- 🟢 **Green** = Positive (celebrate and share)
- 🟡 **Yellow** = Neutral (monitor)"

"In summary, RevuIQ takes the complexity out of review management. Instead of spending hours reading reviews and crafting responses, restaurant owners get:
- ✅ Automatic sentiment analysis

---

## 📝 **Quick Tips for Delivery:**

1. **Speak slowly and clearly** - You have 5 minutes, don't rush
2. **Pause after each section** - Let the visuals sink in
3. **Point to the screen** - "As you can see here..."
4. **Use confident language** - "Our AI automatically...", "With one click..."
5. **Smile and make eye contact** - Show enthusiasm!

## 🎯 **Backup Talking Points (if you have extra time):**

- "We use state-of-the-art NLP models like VADER for sentiment and advanced keyword detection for emotions"
- "The system handles reviews from Google, Yelp, TripAdvisor - all in one place"
- "Restaurant chains can manage multiple locations from a single dashboard"
- "All reviews are auto-approved and immediately visible in analytics"

## ⚠️ **If Something Goes Wrong:**

- **Reviews don't load?** → "We have sample data prepared to show the full functionality"
- **Slow loading?** → "While this loads, let me explain what you'll see..."
- **Error message?** → "This demonstrates why automated systems need human oversight - which is built into our approval workflow"
